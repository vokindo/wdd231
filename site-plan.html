WellNest: Holistic Wellness Tracker - Site Plan

Site Name

WellNest: Holistic Wellness Tracker

This name emphasizes a peaceful, nurturing space where users can track and improve overall wellness. “Tracker” implies personal progress and practical tools.

Possible Domain: wellnesttracker.com

Site Purpose

This website supports users’ mental and emotional wellness by providing tools to track mood, stress, and sleep. It also offers meditations, blogs, and access to professional resources for self-care and growth.

Scenarios

  • How can I track my emotional state and sleep habits consistently?
  • Where can I find meditations or exercises to help reduce daily stress?

Color Scheme

Primary Color – Calm Blue (#2a6f77): Used for headings, buttons, and links.

Accent Color – Soft Mint (#a9d9b8): Used in backgrounds, section dividers, and highlights.
